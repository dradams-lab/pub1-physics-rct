\subsection{Noise Models}

Three noise channels are applied to each gate:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Depolarizing noise}: $\mathcal{E}(\rho) = (1-p)\rho +
        \tfrac{p}{4}\mathbf{I}$, parameterized by error rate $p$.
  \item \textbf{Dephasing (phase damping)}: suppression of off-diagonal
        coherences at rate $\gamma$.
  \item \textbf{Amplitude damping}: energy relaxation at rate $T_1$.
\end{enumerate}
