\subsection{Proof of Proposition~\ref{prop:conservation}}

For a closed system evolving under unitary $U(t)$:
\begin{equation}
  \rho(t) = U(t)\,\rho(0)\,U^\dagger(t).
\end{equation}
Then:
\begin{equation}
  \mathrm{Tr}\bigl[\rho(t)^2\bigr]
    = \mathrm{Tr}\bigl[U\rho(0)U^\dagger U\rho(0)U^\dagger\bigr]
    = \mathrm{Tr}\bigl[\rho(0)^2\bigr],
\end{equation}
since $\mathrm{Tr}[AB] = \mathrm{Tr}[BA]$ and $U^\dagger U = \mathbf{I}$.
Quantum information (purity) is conserved under unitary evolution. $\square$
